HOW TO USE

Use twice daily in the AM + PM, avoiding the eye area.
Apply AM + PM
Wet fingers and massage a dime-sized amount of cleanser evenly over face.
Rinse well and pat dry.
Dispense 2-3 pumps in your palm and massage onto face using fingertips.
Do not rinse.
